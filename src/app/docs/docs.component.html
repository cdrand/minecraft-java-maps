How to find your maps folder

First do a WIN + R and type %appdata%. This will show you your AppData folder.
Open .minecraft folder, and then saves folder.
You should see all of your local maps.

Zip your map

In order to upload your map to the server, you need to zip it.
First make sure you have WinRAR or 7-Zip installed.
WinRAR
Right click on the folder with the map you want to upload. And click add to archive.
7-Zip
You got this!

Upload your map

To upload your map, make sure you are logged in to this website.
Go to the upload page.
